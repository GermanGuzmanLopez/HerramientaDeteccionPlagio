\outl0\strokewidth0 \strokec2 The Metaverse, a virtual reality (VR) space where users can interact with each other and digital objects, is swiftly becoming a reality. As this new world progresses, Artificial Intelligence (AI) is playing an increasingly crucial role in its development. Integrating AI with emerging technologies in the Metaverse opens up new possibilities for immersive experiences that were previously unattainable. This paper examines how AI is combined with technologies such as the Internet of Things, blockchain, Natural Language Processing, virtual reality, Augmented Reality, Mixed Reality, and Extended Reality. One potential advantage of using AI in the Metaverse is the ability to create personalized experiences for individual users based on their behavior and preferences. Another potential benefit is the capability to automate repetitive tasks, freeing up time and resources for more complex and creative endeavors. However, there are also challenges associated with using AI in the Metaverse, such as ensuring user privacy and addressing issues of bias and discrimination. By exploring the potential benefits and challenges of using AI in the Metaverse, including ethical considerations, we can better prepare for this exciting new era of VR. This paper presents a comprehensive review of AI and its integration with other emerging technologies in the Metaverse. As the Metaverse continues to evolve and expand, it will be essential for developers and researchers to stay current with the latest advancements in AI and emerging technologies to fully harness their potential.}
