Artificial Intelligence had become a significant tool in modern technology, enabling people to interact with machines through various methods. Individuals with visual impairments had trouble doing tasks because they were either blind or had poor vision. BVI stood for Blind and Visually Impaired. Solutions had to advance with technology to ensure that individuals could effectively navigate their surroundings and assist them in real-time navigation. The study conducted surveys on visually challenged individuals in the community and aimed to assist them by providing smart gadgets to identify faces, colors, and objects. Moreover, this study emphasized different technologies and methods that were used earlier to help visually impaired people in their day-to-day life.
